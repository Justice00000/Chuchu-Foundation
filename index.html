    The Period Bank Initiative

The Period Bank Initiative is a canadian based foundation that supports the goal of the Girls and Women.

We are committed to building communities where girls and women can fully access period hygiene while living stigma-free at the same time. We do this through period education (interactive games) and provision of sanitary products.

The Challenge

UNICEF reports that over 500 million girls and women worldwide lack access to sanitary pads. In Ghana alone, over 95,000 girls miss school annually due to inadequate menstrual hygiene. In Nigeria, where over 87 million people live below the poverty line, affording disposable sanitary products has become a luxury for many. This challenge has devastating effects on girls' education, with at least 24% of school days missed in Nigeria alone. No girl should have to choose between sanitary pads and food because we firmly believe that period equity is attainable!
